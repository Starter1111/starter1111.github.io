**你好！**  

This is the homepage of Michael. I am currently an Assistant Professor with Tongji University, Shanghai, China. I received the Ph.D. degree in control science and engineering from the East China University of Science and Technology, Shanghai, China, in 2020. From 2017 to 2019, I was a Visiting Scholar with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of California, Riverside, CA, USA. From 2020 to 2023, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher with the East China University of Science and Technology. My current research interests include hybrid systems, multiagent systems, and hybrid networked systems.
